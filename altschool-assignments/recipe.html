Recipe for jellof rice?

This is a simple recipe for making jellof rice, a popular West African dish.


Recipe information

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Number of Servings: 3
  • Difficulty Level: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, blended
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice in cold water and set aside.
  2. In a pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until translucent.
  4. Stir in the blended tomatoes, thyme, curry powder, and salt. Cook for about 10 minutes.
  5. Add the rinsed rice to the pot and stir well to combine.
  6. Pour in enough water to cover the rice by about an inch.
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 30 minutes or until the rice is cooked through.
  8. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, you can add bell peppers or carrots to the recipe.
  • Serve with fried plantains or a side salad for a complete meal.
